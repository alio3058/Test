Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This section explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. What We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, shipping and billing address, phone number
  • Payment Information: Securely processed through third-party payment processors
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Product Usage Data: Router configuration details, performance statistics, and diagnostic data (if you opt in)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process orders and deliver products
  • Provide technical support and firmware updates
  • Improve our website, services, and network hardware
  • Send product announcements, offers, and service updates (with your consent)

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data with trusted partners (e.g., shipping carriers, payment processors, cloud service providers) solely for the purpose of fulfilling services.

We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or respond to law enforcement.

4. Your Rights

Request access to or correction of your data
Withdraw consent or opt-out of communications
Delete your data (subject to contractual or legal obligations)
