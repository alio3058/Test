Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies – Necessary for the operation of our website (e.g., login, shopping cart)

Performance Cookies – Help us understand how users interact with our site

Marketing Cookies – Only used with your consent, to personalize offers and ads

3. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies via your browser settings. Please note, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features.
